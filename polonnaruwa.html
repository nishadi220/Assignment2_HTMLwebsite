Polonnaruwa

Nestled in the lush mountains of Sri Lanka, Mandaram Nuwara is a hidden paradise waiting to be discovered. This remote village, surrounded by tea plantations and rolling hills, offers a unique and tranquil experience for the adventurous traveler.

Getting There

Mandaram Nuwara is not the easiest place to reach, but the journey is part of the adventure. You can take a scenic train ride from Colombo to Hatton, and from there, hire a local driver to take you to Mandaram Nuwara. The winding roads will lead you through stunning landscapes and dense forests, providing a taste of what's to come.

Accomodation

Don't expect luxury resorts in Mandaram Nuwara. Instead, you'll find cozy homestays and guesthouses run by warm and hospitable locals. Staying with them offers a chance to immerse yourself in the village's culture and traditions.

Exploring the Village

Mandaram Nuwara is a haven for nature lovers and hikers. You can embark on guided treks to explore the surrounding mountains and forests. The highlight is the stunning Mandaram Nuwara waterfall, hidden deep within the jungle. The trek to the waterfall takes you through dense vegetation, crossing streams, and ascending rocky terrain, but the reward is worth every step. The pristine waterfall is a breathtaking sight, and you can take a refreshing dip in its cool waters.

Tea Plantation

This region is also famous for its tea plantations. Take a tour of the nearby tea estates, and witness the tea-picking process, which has been a tradition in Sri Lanka for generations. You can even try your hand at plucking tea leaves and enjoy a fresh cup of Ceylon tea.

Local Cuisine

Savor the local cuisine, which often features spicy curries and rice. Don't forget to try the regional specialty, 'rice and curry,' to experience the authentic flavors of Sri Lanka.

Cultural Experience

Engage with the villagers, learn about their traditions, and perhaps even participate in local ceremonies or celebrations. The people of Mandaram Nuwara are friendly and eager to share their way of life with visitors. Mandaram Nuwara may not be on the typical tourist path, but that's precisely what makes it so special. If you're looking for a unique and off-the-beaten-path adventure, Mandaram Nuwara is a destination you won't forget. The village's untouched beauty and the warmth of its people will leave a lasting impression on your heart.
